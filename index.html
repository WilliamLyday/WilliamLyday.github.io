Speed Card game

Goal: Create an application in which two different sessions can connect (such as two different browsers
on the same computer, or two different computers) and play the game of speed with each other. Game
logic is: Shuffle a 52 card deck. Deal 20 cards to both players. Make 4 piles in the middle
of the table with 5, 1, 1, 5 cards in each central pile respectively from left to right. The central middle
piles get flipped face up and each player draws up to 5 cards at any point from their piles of 20.
The goal is to place cards on the two central middle piles until the 20 card player deck is fully depleated.
The first player to completely get rid of their 20 card deck wins. Cards can only be placed on the face up cards in
the middle if their value is +1 or -1 the face value of the middle cards, regardless of suite. (ie 3 of
diamonds can be played on 2 of spades or 4 of hearts). In the case neither player has a valid move the piles of 5
on the sides in the middle are the reserve being flipped over and giving a new set of playable central cards.

Additional Requirements:

The game must be visual and use visual card images.
An integer should display indicating the total number of cards left in each player's deck.
The game must display the winner on both screens when complete.
Upon completing the game, the players must be given the choice to play again.
Play resumes if both vote yes.
Game must be mobile UI friendly.
The game must have a different shuffle of cards each game play.
The game must work within different browsers and be asynchronous,
using websockets to transmit data back and forth via a server.
